About

Built for the job site, not the office.

FieldBourne Digital: Australian job management software for trade businesses.

What FieldBourne Digital means

Field is where your team works, on the road and on the job.
Bourne is where the work flows to: one place everything comes together.

How we're different

We're not an off-the-shelf CRM and we're not a dev shop that builds from scratch every time. FieldBourne Digital is a modular platform: lead capture, job boards, scheduling, and automations, configured for your trade and connected to your existing apps.

Our first live client runs on FieldBourne Companion, a TV aerial franchise. That's the proof. Everything else is built the same way.

Who we work with

Today: solo tradies and small teams (1–10 people) in field service, including TV aerial, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, and solar.

Next: franchise head offices who need visibility across multiple sites without franchisees seeing each other's data.
