Case study

TVMagic Companion

A national TV aerial and satellite franchise — FieldBourne's first live deployment.

The problem

Leads came in via phone, SMS, email and web forms — often while technicians were on the tools. Jobs were tracked in spreadsheets. The franchise owner had no single view of what was open, assigned, or overdue across the team.

What we built

TVMagic Companion runs on FieldBourne's modular platform — configured for aerial and satellite work, not a generic CRM template.

  • Lead board with drag-and-drop status tracking
  • Inbound SMS, email and missed-call capture as leads
  • Calendar booking that auto-creates and assigns jobs
  • Team view — manager sees every tech's pipeline
  • Automated SMS follow-ups and Google review requests
  • Quote composer and job completion workflows
  • Social posting to Facebook and Instagram

Connected tools

SMS and phone via Twilio, inbound email parsing, built-in calendar, Stripe billing for franchise tiers, and Google review link integration. Accounting integrations (Xero) are on the roadmap.

The result

Missed calls are captured as leads automatically. Jobs are assigned with one click. The owner checks live job status from any device — no more chasing technicians for updates.
